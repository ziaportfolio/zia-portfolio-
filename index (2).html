Executive Profile

Zia Iqbal Khan is a seasoned education professional whose career spans front-line secondary teaching, provincial teacher training, and district-level government education administration.

With eight-plus years of progressive leadership across Pakistan’s public education system, he combines systems-level administrative experience with hands-on classroom and training expertise. His most consequential leadership role has been as Assistant District Education Officer (ASDEO, BPS‑16) for Mansehra District — one of Khyber Pakhtunkhwa’s most geographically extensive districts — where he held statutory authority over 90+ government primary schools, directing academic governance, human resource management, School Improvement Planning, teacher development, and community accountability across a four-year tenure.

Academically distinguished across five university qualifications — M.Ed. (Science Education), MA (English), MS (Geology / Geotechnical Engineering), B.Ed., and BS (Geology) — he brings multilingual competency in English, Urdu, and Hindko, and an applied research background that positions him competitively in government, NGO, and international education markets.
